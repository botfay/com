The Importance of Auto-responses and API Telephony

How not to lose a single client.

A client who doesn't receive a response within 5 minutes is 80% likely to go to a competitor. That is why setting up automated responses and telephony integration is critically important.

What Does API Telephony Offer?

  • CRM Integration: Every call is automatically recorded in your system.
  • Smart Distribution: Calls are routed to available managers or handled by a bot.
  • Analytics: You always know how many calls were missed and why.

Auto-responses - The Key to Success

Even if you cannot answer personally, the client must know their request has been received. An automatic message with wait time information or a link to FAQs can save the deal.
